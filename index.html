About Us

Building Trust Through Community Service

A Malawian partnership enterprise dedicated to ethical business practices and meaningful community impact.

Business History

The idea of Tilipo General Suppliers was conceived in 2021, when the founders, Mr. Thomas Mangochi and Mr. Innocent Masauli, began saving and planning for a business that would transform access to goods in communities. Through dedication and persistence, the company was officially launched in 2024.

2021 - Concept Development

Founders began planning and saving for the business idea

2024 - Official Launch

Company officially launched with stationery and groceries focus

2024 - Expansion

Added agricultural supplies division

2025 - Growth Milestones

Acquired delivery vehicles and expanded to two outlets

  • Expand operations across all districts in Malawi
